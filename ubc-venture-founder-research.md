# UBC Venture Founder Program - Research & Benefits

## Program Overview

**Venture Founder** is a flagship 16-week accelerator program by Innovation UBC that supports early-stage ventures from UBC students, faculty, staff, and researchers.

### Program Duration & Structure
- 16-week intensive program
- Experiential workshops covering venture validation, business model development, and leadership training
- Hands-on support from experienced mentors throughout the journey

## Key Benefits for Startups

### 1. World-Class Mentorship Network
- **250+ experienced mentors** in the Mentor Network
- Direct access to startup entrepreneurs, industry experts, and UBC alumni
- Personalized guidance to navigate challenges, refine strategies, and accelerate growth
- Mentors encourage founders to push boundaries of their thinking

### 2. Proven Track Record & Network
- **800 ventures supported** through the program since inception
- **$221M+ in funding** raised by alumni ventures
- **50 cohorts completed** as of Fall 2025 (milestone cohort)
- **275 UBC spinoff companies** created
- **$13 billion in sales** generated by UBC-supported ventures
- Connections to investors, partners, customers, and fellow founders

### 3. Structured Learning & Resources
- Workshops on:
  - Venture validation and customer discovery
  - Business model development and refinement
  - Financial literacy and unit economics
  - Team building and leadership capacity
  - Storytelling and pitch development
  - Operational topics for establishing viable startups
- Resources and networks needed to succeed
- Foundation for turning concepts into market-ready ventures

### 4. Strategic Ecosystem Access
- Deep integration with Vancouver's entrepreneurship ecosystem
- Access to UBC's innovation infrastructure
- Connections to corporate, hospitality, sports, and tech communities
- Pipeline to additional UBC programs (HATCH Venture Builder, Lab2Market, etc.)

## Why Startups Choose UBC Venture Founder

### Compelling Arguments from Success Stories:

1. **First Money In**: For many ventures (like AVA), UBC provides the first institutional funding that validates and kickstarts the business

2. **Avoid Costly Mistakes**: Mentorship from 250+ entrepreneurs who've built and scaled businesses helps founders navigate challenges without expensive trial-and-error

3. **Accelerate Growth**: Structured program compresses learning curves - what might take years to figure out independently happens in 16 weeks

4. **Network Effects**: 800 alumni ventures create powerful network effects - introductions, partnerships, customer referrals, and investor connections

5. **Proven Playbook**: 50 cohorts of experience means the program has refined its approach to what actually works for early-stage ventures

6. **De-Risk the Journey**: Validation support and mentor feedback helps stress-test assumptions before expensive commitments

7. **Ecosystem Integration**: Deep connections to Vancouver's business community, investors, and corporate partners

8. **Credibility Signal**: Being part of UBC's innovation ecosystem signals legitimacy to customers, partners, and future investors

## What Makes It Different from Funding Requests

Unlike traditional accelerators that focus primarily on investment:
- **Focus on Foundations**: Building sustainable business models, not just pitching for capital
- **Mentorship-Driven**: Direct access to experienced entrepreneurs, not just investor feedback
- **Longer Engagement**: 16 weeks provides time for meaningful progress vs. demo days
- **Resource Access**: Workshops, tools, networks, and UBC infrastructure beyond just money
- **Community Building**: Cohort model creates lasting peer networks and collaboration opportunities
